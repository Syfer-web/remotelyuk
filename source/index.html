FAQs

Common questions about our remote staffing service

How quickly can I start with a remote worker?
Most clients are matched with a remote worker and up and running within 2–5 working days of their free consultation. Once you've had your initial call, the process moves swiftly — we handle the matching and handover so you can start straight away.

Where are your remote workers based?
Our assistants are sourced internationally and go through Remotely's comprehensive vetting, training, and screening programme before working with any client. Every assistant passes English language assessments, background checks, and skills tests — ensuring a consistently high standard of work regardless of location.

Do you offer long-term contracts?
Yes — long-term contracts are available and can be discussed during your free consultation. We also offer flexible month-to-month plans with no minimum term. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel monthly plans with 30 days' notice at any time.

What tasks can a remote worker handle?
Our remote workers cover a wide range of tasks including email and inbox management, calendar and diary scheduling, data entry, social media posting, cold calling, lead generation, outreach email campaigns, CRM updates, and general admin support. We'll tailor the task list to what your business actually needs.

Can I change my remote worker plan?
Absolutely. You can switch between the Admin and Sales Support plans, or add adhoc days at any time. Just let us know and we'll adjust your support accordingly — usually within a few working days.

How much does a remote worker cost in the UK?
Remotely's remote staffing services start from £650 per month for admin support and £850 per month for full sales and admin support. Adhoc admin cover is available from £45 per day and adhoc sales cover from £65 per day — no minimum commitment required.

What if I'm not happy with my assistant?
We'll resolve any issues swiftly. If a change of assistant is needed, we handle the rematch at no extra cost and with minimal disruption to your workflow. Client satisfaction is our priority — and our 97% satisfaction rate reflects that.

What tools do your remote workers use?
Our remote workers are proficient in most common business tools including Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Slack, Asana, Trello, and more. If you use something specific, just let us know during your consultation.
